Antipolo Residences

3 Bathrooms 3 Bedrooms 1 Garage

Antipolo Residences is the modern housing subdivision in Antipolo. It is located at Brgy. Bayugo Buliran, Antipolo City. A mixture of an affordable yet elegant economic housing project in Antipolo. The project is strategically located few kilometers away from Antipolo Miraculous Church and the Municipality of Antipolo. It is an accessible getaway for people who wish to take a breather from the heat and dust of the metro and feel the refreshing mountain breeze. It’s also a wonderful retreat from the stresses of work at the end of the day, and is just an hour’s drive away from Ortigas Center.

A good reason to consider living in Antipolo Residences is how there are a few established public and private schools in the area, like: Philippine International College, La Salle College, and Assumption College; which is only few kilometers away from the proposed subdivision. Aside from these, Antipolo Public School is also near the project. Another important commercial landmark in the nearby vicinity are the hospitals and clinics that are always available anytime. Business establishments such as commercial banks, department stores like SM Masinag, Robinson’s Place Mall, Super Metro Mall, Victory Mall are also within the vicinity. Supermarkets like SM Hypermart, Shopwise, Budgetlande, Super Palengke, Super8, Puregold, and the newly opened Rustan’s Supermarket and Antipolo Public Market are all accessible via public tricycle and public transportation like jeepneys. The site is also about 18 kilometers away from Ortigas Center. Major communication lines and internet connections are also available in the area.

One major consideration for choosing to live in the City of Antipolo is that there is peace and order. The city has low crime rates which can be attributed to the City Administration’s campaign against illegal activities in the area. With this condition, the development of CITI TOWN DEVELOPMENT CORPORATION serves as a grand invitation for another comfortable residential experience in Antipolo.
