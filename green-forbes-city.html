Green Forbes City

3 Bathrooms 3 Bedrooms 1 Garage

Green Forbes City is a decent economic and socialized housing project located in Brgy. Hugo Perez, Trece Martirez City, Cavite. Under B.P. 220, about 80% of the total saleable lots and at least 20% shall be allotted to socialized housing. The houses in this project are affordable yet elegant. It is easily accessible to schools, offices, and other commercial establishments just within its proximity.

Price packages are made affordable to cater to the needs of local and migrant families from the greater Manila area and Cavite. With a total of 978 sellable lots and housing units with a gross area of 93,087 sqm., Green Forbes City is complete with warm and welcoming amenities. Reflecting comfort, accessibility, and convenience for an urban lifestyle. Green Forbes City is a place where everything is just around the corner.
