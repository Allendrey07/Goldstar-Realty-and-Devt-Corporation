More about Goldstar Realty

Goldstar Realty and Development Corporation (“Goldstar”) is one of Luzon’s real estate developers that conducts and aims to be one of the most preferred real estate business nationwide.

Goldstar was given juridical personality by the Securities and Exchange Commission on March 8, 2005 with registration No. CS200503563 in 2006. Goldstar’s first launch, Karlaville Parkhomes is the first horizontal mass housing project catering to clients in Trece Martirez, Cavite. Because of the success of the project and its increasing investor confidence, the company saw the opportunity for developing even more horizontal housing development, thus the emergence of Green Forbes Residences (Bulacan), Green Forbes City (Cavite) and Tagaytay forbes Residences.

Goldstar’s success is driven by its people and their commitment to do business ethically–by operating responsibly, developing projects with excellence that goes beyond expectations, applying innovative engineering and design technology, and, taking advantage of new opportunities for profitable growth.




Our Brand Story

Goldstar Realty and Development Corporation ("Goldstar") is one of Luzon's real estate developers that aims to be one of the most preferred real estate businesses nationwide.

Goldstar was given juridical personality by the Securities and Exchange Commission on March 8, 2005 with registration No. C$200503563 in 2006.

Its first launch, Karlaville Parkhomes, also happens to be the first horizontal mass housing project catering to clients in Trece Martirez, Cavite. Because of the project's success, and its increasing investor confidence, the company saw the opportunity for developing even more horizontal housing developments. Thus, Green Forbes Residences (Bulacan), Green Forbes City (Cavite) and Tagaytay Forbes Residences came to be.

Goldstar's success is driven by its people and their commitment to do business ethically: by operating responsibly, developing projects with excellence that goes beyond expectations, applying innovative engineering and design technology, and taking advantage of new opportunities for profitable growth.

By developing comforting residential projects, Goldstar inspires the Filipino people to achieve their dreams. It is Goldstar's mission not just to build a housing structure, but to also build a home and a community.
