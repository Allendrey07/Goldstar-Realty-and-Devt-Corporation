Green Forbes Residences
Phase 3

3 Bathrooms 3 Bedrooms 1 Garage

Green Forbes Residences Phase 3 is an economic housing project located in Loma De Gato, Marilao Bulacan. It has a strategic location that is a few kilometers away from the National Shrine of the Divine Mercy in Marilao; and it is also accessible from NLEX, which is 15 minutes away from the project and, 35 minutes going to Balintawak.

The project is located near the public market and a few schools--like Loma de Gato Elementary School. Aside from these, aspiring residents may feel safer knowing that there are also other projects from well-known developers nearby, as well as a private hospital called "Kayrus Hospital" within the vicinity. Other near commercial landmarks include the following:

  • Puregold (1 km away)
  • Muzon Market
  • Savemore (5 mins away)
  • Philippine Arena (15 mins away)
  • SM San Jose (20-25 mins away)
  • SM Marilao (which is just on the other side of the project)

All accessible via public transportation.

Of the many establishments near the project, the proposed "New Manila International Airport" or NMIA that's to be built in Bulacan may become very advantageous to those living in the area. In this proposed contract, it is stated that there will be an 8.4-kilometer tollway that connects the airport to NLEX through Marilao, which could make Metro Manila more accessible for Bulacan residents.
