Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements for the housing loan?

List of Requirements

  1. 2pcs. 2x2 Latest Pictures
  2. Info-sheet/sketch of residences (properly filled-up)
  3. Marriage Certificate and Spouse ID (if married)
  4. Birth Certificate/CENOMAR (if single)
  5. Latest Residences Certificate
  6. 2 Valid Gov't Issued ID Card
  7. Proof of Billing (Meralco/PLDT Bill)
  8. Latest 2 Years of Income Tax Return
  9. Latest 6 Months Payslip
  10. Latest 6 Months Bank Statements
  11. Post Dated Checks for Down Payment and Monthly Amortization
  12. Latest Original Certificate of Employment w/ Compensation and Notarized at least 3 yrs. Work Tenure

For Overseas Contract Worker

  1. Latest Employment Contract if Borrower Abroad
  2. Consularized Special Power of Attorney if Borrower is Abroad
  3. Passport/Arrival/Departure and Eticket
  4. Working/Residence Permit ID and ID of Attorney in Fact
  5. Proof of Remittances

For Self-Employed

  1. Business Name
  2. Business Permit
  3. Latest Audited Financial Statement
  4. Income Tax Withheld/Tax Receipt
  5. Latest 6 Months Bank Statement
Which financial institutions are accredited for housing loan services?
  • MBTC (Metropolitan Bank & Trust Company)
  • RCBC (Rizal Commercial Banking Corporation)
Are there additional fees or charges beyond the loan amount that need to be considered?
Yes, there are additional expenses including bank charges, move-in fees, and utility costs such as water and electricity.
What is the estimated duration for the moving process, and what is the typical processing timeframe for permits?
It will necessitate a period of one week subsequent to the signing.
How many months is the process once the requirements are completed?
The process typically spans two to three months, involving document processing and title annotation (RD).
