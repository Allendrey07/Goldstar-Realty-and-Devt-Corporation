Tagaytay Forbes

Goldstar Realty and Development Corp. introduces the, a Modern American-inspired house & lot package. It is located in the middle of the city's highlands and ridges with the iconic view of the world-renowned Taal Volcano. Tagaytay Forbes is an ideal place to unwind and reminisce about the good times--a perfect place for some peace and serenity. The project and the city itself, boast of its cool weather and fine climate all year round because of the city’s high elevation, which averages 634 meters above sea level. The project is also strategically located near tourist spots, and good local souvenir stores and restaurants.

Tagaytay Forbes Residences is just perfect for the whole family, as its accessibility is never compromised since it is located in one of the city’s most visited places. The project is situated along Amadeo Road, Barangay Kaybagal Central, Tagaytay City, Cavite. This location is near the famous SM Sky Ranch Amusement Park, Ayala Malls Serin, and the People’s Park in the Sky (at Mt. Sungay, the highest point in Cavite at 709 meters)—all certified tourist attractions.

The Tagaytay Forbes Residences offers has one of the lowest reservation fees to be ever offered--given the scale of this residential project development. The low reservation fee is definitely an advantage for interested investors. It is also budget-friendly as a downpayment is payable at a maximum of 12 months installment with zero interest. This payment scheme helps interested investors properly plan out their finances.
